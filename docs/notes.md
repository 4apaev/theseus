## time managment for multiplayer
`absolute` time is measured at `stations`,
while `relative` time is measured on `ships`.
a mechanism is needed to account for this difference,
as travel from `sol` to `barnard` will take several years.
the question is how to present this information to players,
assuming there are enough activities on the `stations` for them to engage in.
